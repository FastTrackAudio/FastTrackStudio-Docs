## Available Tones

- **Electric Guitar**: Classic and modern guitar tones for various genres
- **Bass Guitar**: Deep, rich bass tones for any style
- **Synthesizer**: Analog and digital synth presets for electronic music
- **Acoustic Instruments**: Natural acoustic instrument emulations

## How to Use

Each tone in the library includes detailed information about:

- The equipment used to create the tone
- Recommended settings for different playing styles
- Tips for customization to suit your needs
